Federal Contractor Completes Chiller Plant at DC Veterans Hospital

Hamilton Pacific Chamberlain has successfully completed the installation and commissioning of a new chiller plant at the Washington DC VA Medical Center. The project involved full mechanical demolition, new chiller unit procurement, piping, and integration with existing HVAC controls — all performed within an occupied, operational hospital environment. The completed system significantly improves cooling reliability and energy efficiency across the facility.
