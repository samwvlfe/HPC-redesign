Company Overview
Who We Are

Hamilton Pacific Chamberlain (HPC), LLC is a verified Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) providing premier general construction, repair, and modernization services to federal facilities. We specialize in interior renovations, administrative and clinical support space upgrades, and MEP-intensive projects performed under firm-fixed-price, design-bid-build contracts within occupied VA facilities.
